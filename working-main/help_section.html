How do I request a medicine refill?

Go to the Medicine section, find the medicine you need, and click "Refill Now". Your caretaker will receive a notification and can approve the refill.

When should I press the SOS button?

Use SOS only in emergencies. Press the red "SOS EMERGENCY" button or say "SOS" to immediately alert your caretaker.

What languages does the voice assistant support?

English, Hindi, Marathi, Gujarati, Tamil, Bengali, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi, Urdu, and Odia. Change your language in the Voice Assistant settings.

Where can I see my health vitals?

Your vitals (heart rate, blood pressure, blood sugar) are displayed on the "My Day" home dashboard. They update as you track them.

How do I view my medical history and allergies?

Open "My Profile" section. You'll find complete medical history, allergy warnings, medications, and personal information.
